Marley & Me

This is an American comedy-drama film.The film stars Owen Wilson and Jennifer Aniston as the owners of Marley, a Labrador retriever. The first half of the movie is just a routine life of the couple who has a dog named Marley. Initially I felt the movie is not bad. But the lines given by the owner when the dog dies made me change my decision about the movie: "A dog has no use for fancy cars, big homes, or designer clothes. A water log stick will do just fine. A dog doesn't care if your rich or poor, clever or dull, smart or dumb. Give him your heart and he'll give you his."
